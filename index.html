THE FUNNGRO WAY / PROCESS

Five steps from first tap to first payout.

Follow our structured flow to verify your profile, secure brand projects, complete milestones, and collect earnings.

01

Download the app.

Download Funngro from the App Store or Google Play. Complete your profile, select your interest areas, and verify your parent's consent.

  • Profile build is 100% free
  • Safe, COPPA compliant
  • Parent dashboard link sent instantly
02

Pick a brand campaign.

Browse open campaigns from companies. Filter by category, duration, and rewards. Apply to campaigns matching your skills and schedule.

  • Top startup & MNC campaigns
  • Verified payment milestones
  • No prior experience required
03

Do the work.

Follow campaign briefs step-by-step. Submit screenshots or proof of completion directly through the app interface. Review criteria are transparent.

  • Real-time milestone tracker
  • Peer-to-peer feedback chats
  • 24/7 dedicated support
04

Get paid.

Earn earnings for every verified action. Payments are credited directly into your Funngro wallet and can be withdrawn directly into a bank account.

  • Direct-to-bank UPI transfers
  • Track earnings in real-time
  • No processing or platform fees
05

Grow your ladder.

Complete more campaigns to build your reputation score. Higher scores unlock exclusive high-paying projects, team leader roles, and badges.

  • Global leaderboard status
  • Digital portfolio resume link
  • Exclusive invite-only projects
